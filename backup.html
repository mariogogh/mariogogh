Caliente.
A Reggaeton Playlist

2021


Caliente is a playlist of the latest Reggaeton hits, which seeks to satisfy fans of the genre as well as attract new ones.

It is a Final project of course “Decoding Identities” taught by Carlos Bocai & Julia Aguiar at Aprender Design.

Team: Luma Cabral, Mariogogh & Tullio Cunha.
