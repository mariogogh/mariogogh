EVINO · 2016

A friendly approach to
sell wine

ABOUT


The wine industry in Brazil is still a niche market, often focused on people at an older age and that is part of elite social groups. Evino embraced the mission of making the wine experience accessible to more people, innovating to make wine the go-to drink of anyone.

Evino wanted to bring a friendly approach to present the wine selection and break the barrier people usually have when first entering the wine world. Their goal was also to make the brand clearer across the channels.

PRISM.AI · 2019 ⏤ 2020

Turning any video into a
data source

ABOUT

Prism.Ai is an early-stage Silicon Valley startup with offices in San Francisco and an international development team in Kyiv, Ukraine. Their mission is to bring the latest advances in computer vision, using machine learning and artificial intelligence, with the data gathered from the cameras and sensors all around us.

In this project, I was responsible for creating concepts and a complete user flow, analyzing different use cases for a new computer vision application.
