About me


As a growing product designer, I am interested in contributing to digital experiences that are thoughtfully concieved and refined in execution. My favourite type of professional projects involve translating meticulously crafted details into deliberate design systems.

1. On the side, I enjoy creating personal work that represents timely content in visually expressive and distinct ways.

2. As I continue in my career, I am interested in investigating the intricacies of the human condition through design. In doing so, I believe we can collaboratively create beautiful and enduring artifacts that can provide people with genuine value, and hopefully a little bit of truth 3.
