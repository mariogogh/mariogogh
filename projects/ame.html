ABOUT


Ame is a platform that simplifies payments and offers discounts and cashback in local stores and digital marketplaces. Seeking to enhance its market positioning and deliver an outstanding user experience, Ame approached Tropikal / Accenture with the challenge of transforming its app into a super app. The main objective was to enable a more personalized experience for each user through a vision strategy from design for a user journey evolution via the services portfolio and Ame Digital Products.

A Super App also needs a great Rewards Program.


We also helped Ame with a rewards program by creating a concept for a Game format that was tested, detailed in user flows a component library, and then developed.
